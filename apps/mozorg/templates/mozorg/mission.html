The Mozilla Difference

Mozilla is a proudly non-profit organization dedicated to keeping the power of the Web in people's hands. We're a global community of users, contributors and developers working to innovate on your behalf. When you use Firefox or any Mozilla products, you become a part of that community, helping us build a brighter future for the Web.

To find out more about who we are, where we came from and where we want to take the Web, please watch the video above.

Read the Mozilla Manifesto to learn more about the values and principles that guide the pursuit of our mission.
