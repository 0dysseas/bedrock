Firefox on your mobile device

To find out if Firefox works on your phone, choose your manufacturer from the list below.

Firefox is available for Android phones (2.1 and above). Below is a list of tested devices compatible with Firefox.

  • Firefox is compatible with Android 2.1 and above devices.
  • Installation requires about 17 MB internal or SD card storage and 512 MB of RAM

Your feedback in this short survey will help Mozilla determine and widen our coverage and support of Firefox on a variety of Android based mobile devices.
